📖 USER GUIDE — SLAP-GAP AIR NAV

1. GETTING STARTED

Open the app via your browser or home screen shortcut. Enter the password when prompted. The map loads centred on Bangladesh. The sidebar is on the right (desktop) or bottom (iPad). Collapse/expand it with the arrow tab button.

2. MAP LAYERS

Use the Map Layer buttons to switch between: 🗺️ Normal (OpenStreetMap), 🛰️ Satellite (Esri), ⛰️ Topo (terrain), 🌑 Dark (night), 🗾 ESRI Topo. The quick-toggle button on the top-left of the map switches between Normal and Satellite instantly.

3. ADDING WAYPOINTS

By tapping the map: Tap any point → a confirmation dialog appears → tap YES, CREATE → enter name and optional notes.
By coordinates: Use the Jump to Coordinates section → enter Lat/Lon in DMS format → tap GO / ADD WAYPOINT.
Waypoints appear as coloured markers. Each marker type has a symbol: ● Default (red), H Helipad (blue), ★ Important (gold), ◆ Checkpoint (silver), ▲ Marker (white), ■ Airport (green).

4. WAYPOINT LIST & SEARCH

Tap 📋 LIST next to Waypoints. A modal opens showing all waypoints. Use the search bar at the top to filter by name instantly. Each entry shows Edit, Delete, Select, and (if plates exist) a 📄 Plates button.

5. EDITING A WAYPOINT

Tap a marker on the map → popup appears → tap Edit. Or use the 📋 LIST modal. The edit screen lets you change: Name, Latitude, Longitude, Marker Type, Info/Notes, and add Approach Plates (PDFs).

6. APPROACH PLATES (PDFs)

In the Edit Waypoint modal, scroll to the 📄 APPROACH PLATES section. Tap ➕ ADD PLATE (PDF) to pick one or more PDF files. They are saved inside the app.
Viewing: From the map popup → tap 📄 N Plate(s). From the Waypoints List → tap 📄 Plates. A full-screen viewer opens with PREV / NEXT buttons to navigate between plates. All PDF pages are shown. Tap ✖ CLOSE to return.

7. BUILDING A ROUTE

Select 2 or more waypoints by tapping them (they highlight blue) or using 🎯 Sel in the list. Tap ⛓ ROUTE to draw the route line. The Route Summary below shows each leg: distance, bearing, and ETA. Enter your speed in knots in the SPEED field for accurate ETAs. Tap ✖ CLEAR ROUTE to reset.

8. SAVING & LOADING ROUTES

After building a route, tap 💾 SAVE ROUTE → enter a name → confirm. View saved routes via 📂 SAVED ROUTES. Each saved route can be edited (rename), shown on map, or deleted. Routes remember the waypoint IDs used — if waypoints are deleted, re-add them before showing the route.

9. FLYING MODE & GPS

Tap 🚁 FLY ON to activate GPS tracking. A helicopter icon follows your position. GPS Speed and Course are displayed live. Tap ⏹ FLY OFF to stop. Note: GPS requires HTTPS (your GitHub Pages link) and location permission granted in the browser.

10. DIRECT-TO NAVIGATION

Select a waypoint from the Direct-To Waypoint dropdown → tap ➤ DIRECT-TO. A magenta dashed line draws from your position to the target waypoint, updating live with distance, bearing, and ETA. Tap ⏹ STOP to end.

11. MOVING TRACK PROJECTION

In Flying Mode, tap 📡 TRACK ON. A green dashed line projects your 20-minute track based on current GPS speed and course. The panel shows projected distance, course, and speed. Tap 📡 TRACK OFF to hide.

12. AREA MANAGEMENT

Tap ✚ CREATE AREA to draw a polygon or circular restricted/operational area. Enter corner coordinates or choose a centre waypoint and diameter. Areas are colour-coded and labelled on the map. Use the Show / Hide Areas panel to toggle individual area visibility.

13. MEASUREMENT TOOL

Tap 📏 MEASURE on the map. Two draggable endpoints appear. Drag them to measure the distance and reciprocal bearings between any two points on the map. Tap ⏹ STOP to close.

14. PRE-FLIGHT TILE CACHE (OFFLINE USE)

Expand the Pre-flight Cache section. Tap 📦 CACHE AREA → draw a rectangle on the map over your operating area → select zoom levels and map layers → tap ⬇ DOWNLOAD NOW. All tiles in that area are saved for offline use. Useful before flights in low-connectivity areas.

15. IMPORT / EXPORT

💾 SAVE: Saves all waypoints and areas to this browser's local storage.
📤 EXPORT: Opens the export menu. Choose what to export — All, Waypoints Only, Areas Only, Selected items, Custom mix, or 🛣️ Share Routes (exports selected routes with their waypoints). Tick "Include Approach Plates PDFs" to include PDF data (increases file size).
📥 IMPORT: Paste exported JSON into the text area and tap IMPORT. Waypoints, areas, and routes are all restored, including PDFs if they were included in the export.

16. PRINT FLIGHT BRIEF

Tap 🖨️ PRINT BRIEF. Fill in mission details, weather, NOTAM, and special notes. The current route is automatically included in the briefing. Tap 🖨️ PRINT / SAVE PDF to open the print dialog.

17. TIPS FOR iPAD USE

• The sidebar docks at the bottom in portrait/landscape iPad mode — tap the handle to collapse/expand it.
• PDF approach plates show all pages when opened in the viewer.
• Pre-cache tiles before flight for offline map use.
• Add this app to your iPad Home Screen for a full-screen PWA experience.
• Use Safari on iPad for best GPS and PDF compatibility.
